Fireworks Safety Tips

Stay Safe While Enjoying Fireworks

Fireworks are exciting, but safety comes first. Follow these tips:

  • Always read and follow the instructions on the fireworks.
  • Keep a bucket of water or a hose nearby in case of fire.
  • Only light fireworks outdoors in a clear area away from buildings.
  • Never point or throw fireworks at another person.
