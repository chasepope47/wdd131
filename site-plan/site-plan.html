Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to educate and inspire visitors about the beauty of fireworks. It showcases their history, safety tips, and modern displays as well as some of my own experiences.

Audience

This site targets fireworks enthusiasts, families interested in learning about safety, and those curious about cultural and historical uses of fireworks.

Dynamic Elements

The site includes a JavaScript-driven "Fun Facts" generator, a responsive navigation menu, and a fireworks gallery with filtering functionality.
